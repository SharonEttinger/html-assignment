Bands

  • No Te Va Gustar

    No Te Va Gustar, also known by their initials NTVG, is an Uruguayan rock band formed in 1994 in Montevideo.
    Genre: rock.
    Actual members: Emiliano Brancciari, Guzmán «Chule» Silveira, Diego Bartaburu, Denis Ramos, Mauricio Ortiz, Martín Gil, Pablo «Bambino» Coniberti, Francisco Nasser
    Favorite album: Aunque cueste ver el sol, 2004.

  • Foo Fighters

    Foo Fighters is an American rock band formed in Seattle in 1994.
    Genre: rock, grunge.
    Actual members: Dave Grohl, Nate Mendel, Pat Smear, Chris Shiflett, Rami Jaffee.
    Favorite album: Concrete and Gold, 2017.

  • The 1975

    The 1975 are an English pop rock band formed in 2002 in Wilmslow, Cheshire.
    Genre: pop, indie rock.
    Actual members: Matty Healy, Adam Hann, Ross MacDonald, George Daniel.
    Favorite album: Being Funny in a Foreign Language, 2022.

  • Arctic Monkeys

    Arctic Monkeys are an English rock band formed in Sheffield in 2002.
    Genre: indie rock.
    Actual members: Alex Turner, Jamie Cook, Matt Helders, Nick O'Malley.
    Favorite album: AM, 2013.
